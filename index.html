About Me

Who am I

Hello, I'm Nitesh Maurya, a final-year B.Tech undergraduate student at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IITGN) , majoring in Electrical Engineering. Since my childhood, I've been deeply passionate about electronics, and this fervor has only grown stronger during my academic journey. From my first year of B.Tech, I've been playing with arduino, nodeMCU and other microcontrollers. I've had the privilege to work on a range of exciting projects, including designing a Convolutional Neural Network in System Verilog, developing an Automatic Library Evaluation Framework, and creating a Pipelined Five-stage three-channel Convolution hardware using System Verilog. Alongside my studies, I was an active member of the technical team in Electronica, Amalthea, where I've crafted challenging questions for competitions related to analog and digital electronics. When I'm not immersed in the world of electronics, I love spending my some time playing outdoor games like cricket, volleyball, and kabaddi, and challenging my mind with indoor games like chess. I'm always eager to learn, grow, and apply my skills to tackle real-world problems, and I look forward to a promising future in the field of engineering.
